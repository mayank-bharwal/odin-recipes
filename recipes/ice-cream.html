Strawberry Ice Cream

Description

Simple and easy recipe for making a delicious and yummy Strawberry Ice Cream

Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Â¾ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. In a blender or food processor, puree the strawberries until smooth.
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, and vanilla extract until the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Add the strawberry puree to the cream mixture and stir until well combined.
  4. If desired, add lemon juice to enhance the strawberry flavor and a pinch of salt.
  5. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  6. Transfer the churned ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 4 hours, or until firm.
  7. Let the ice cream sit at room temperature for a few minutes before serving to soften slightly.

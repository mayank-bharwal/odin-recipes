Cornmeal Cookie

Description

Simple and easy recipe for making a delicious and yummy Cookie

Cornmeal Cookies Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cornmeal
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ¼ cup milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together cornmeal, flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract until well combined.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Add the milk and mix until the dough comes together.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.
  9. Cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
